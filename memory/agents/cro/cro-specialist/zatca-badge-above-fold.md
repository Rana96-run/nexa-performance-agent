Every LP variant carries the **ZATCA compliance badge above the fold.** It is not
optional and not a designer's judgement call — it ships on every page, every test.

**Why:** ZATCA compliance is the core trust signal for Saudi accounting buyers;
below-fold or missing kills conversion and credibility.

**How to apply:** state it in the 8-section brief; `ui-ux-designer` places it above
the fold; `developer` confirms it renders before sign-off. No exceptions.
