# Audience Segments — Psychographic Model

Source: Psychographic and Behavioral Audience Analysis (Notion, Nov 2025, Vetted by Mohamed)

## OCEAN Personality Model (Big Five)

Use this framework to tailor content for different audience types:

| Trait | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| **O** (Openness) | Creative, curious, variety-seeking | Practical, traditional, routine-preferring |
| **C** (Conscientiousness) | Organized, disciplined, goal-oriented | Spontaneous, flexible, easy-going |
| **E** (Extraversion) | Outgoing, energetic, seeks stimulation | Reserved, solitary, prefers quiet |
| **A** (Agreeableness) | Compassionate, trusting, cooperative | Analytical, detached, competitive |
| **N** (Neuroticism) | Anxious, sensitive, emotionally reactive | Calm, resilient, emotionally stable |

## 4 Audience Segments for Content Strategy

### 1. Innovators (High Openness + High Extraversion)
- **Content style:** Creative, novel, forward-thinking
- **Hooks:** "New", "Exclusive", "Join the movement"
- **Format:** Visual, interactive, video-first
- **Qoyod angle:** New features, cloud innovation, modern accounting

### 2. Connectors (Low Openness + High Extraversion)
- **Content style:** Social, trend-driven, visual CTAs
- **Hooks:** Social proof, testimonials, community
- **Format:** Short-form, shareable, case studies
- **Qoyod angle:** Customer stories, team collaboration features

### 3. Analysts (High Openness + Low Extraversion)
- **Content style:** Depth, facts, data-driven, long-form
- **Hooks:** "Complete guide", "Deep dive", "Everything you need"
- **Format:** Long articles, whitepapers, comparison tables
- **Qoyod angle:** Feature comparisons, technical guides, API docs

### 4. Conservers (Low Openness + Low Extraversion)
- **Content style:** Familiar, trust cues, established authority
- **Hooks:** "Trusted by", "Certified", "Proven"
- **Format:** FAQs, step-by-step guides, compliance docs
- **Qoyod angle:** ZATCA compliance, Saudi regulations, security

## Regulatory Focus (Promotion vs Prevention)

### Promotion-Focused (Gain-seeking)
- Message: Emphasize gains, opportunities, growth
- Example: "Grow your business with automated invoicing"
- CTR lift: +30% when matched

### Prevention-Focused (Risk-averse)
- Message: Emphasize safety, compliance, risk avoidance
- Example: "Protect your business — stay ZATCA compliant"
- CTR lift: +28% when matched

### Cross-targeting penalty: -12% to -15% when mismatched

## Platform-Specific Audience Data (2025)

### X (Twitter)
- **Age:** Mainly 25-44 years old
- **Geography:** ~50% Saudi Arabia, followed by Yemen and Egypt
- **Gender:** Relatively balanced
- **Behavior:** Strong profile visits (26.8K from 928 verified followers) = active interest beyond passive scrolling
- **Content preference:** Arabic educational and product-related updates; practical accounting content aligned with Saudi regulations

### LinkedIn
- **Seniority:** 47% Senior/Manager level = decision-makers who can approve purchases
- **Departments:** Accounting, Finance, Sales — perfect B2B audience for accounting SaaS
- **Behavior:** Engages selectively with high-value content; evaluates companies before partnerships/purchases
- **Growth:** +22.1% (5.8K new followers in 2025) = consistent interest from the right professional segment

### TikTok
- **Traffic:** 95%+ from "For You" page = content consistently pushed to new audiences (not limited to followers)
- **Behavior:** Learning what accounting problems look like, but not yet connecting to Qoyod as the solution
- **Demographics:** SME owners and young professionals (target for 2026)

### Instagram
- **Behavior:** Steady follower growth = consistent brand discovery (not viral spikes)
- **Engagement:** Static posts and simple visuals drive most interactions; Stories have high completion rate
- **Audience type:** Top-of-funnel; low-effort visual consumption

### Facebook
- **Demographics:** Wide demographic range; familiar platform for SME community
- **Behavior:** Passive consumption; responds to clarity and practicality
- **Groups:** Underutilized potential for SME owners and accountants community building
