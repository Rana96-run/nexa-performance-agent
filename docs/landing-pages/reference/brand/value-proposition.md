## Strategic Positioning — Trojan Horse (Compliance-First)

**Use case:** Phase 2 e-invoicing compliance.
**Differentiator:** Only Qoyod pairs ZATCA-compliant tools with **real, trained accountants**, in Arabic, with local support, and sets everything up for the customer.
**Market thesis:** Saudi SMBs urgently need to comply with ZATCA Phase 2. Most don't know how. Qoyod captures this demand by **solving compliance first, then expanding to full financial management**.

### Unified talking points (every business case)

1. **The Compliance Trap** — Most SMBs still use non-compliant tools or don't realize Phase 2 applies to them. Qoyod's approach: start with compliance, then help them run the business. Everything (POS, bookkeeping, payroll, accounting) is configured to pass ZATCA validation from Day 1.
2. **The Human Expert Edge** — Compliance rules are confusing. Qoyod wins not just by offering the tech but by pairing it with a SOCPA-certified expert who gets it right for the customer. Other tools leave users to figure it out; Qoyod gives them a person who configures the setup, validates the flows, and trains them in Arabic.
3. **The Fear Profile** — SMB owners are afraid of getting fined, messing up filings, or not knowing if they're compliant. Accountants are afraid of cleaning up client mistakes, getting blamed for errors they didn't make, and wasting time on rework.
4. **The Firm Scale Problem** — Accounting firms want to scale advisory, not do cleanup. Qoyod keeps client books clean daily, works inside the Qoyod platform (not a Google Sheet on the side), and follows local workflows, SLAs, and permissions.
5. **Arabic-First and Saudi-Specific** — English-only global tools lose the trust of non-technical users. Qoyod is designed in Arabic, for ZATCA, with local Saudi-based onboarding and support.

2. **Sales Team Reaches Out** — Qoyod's sales operations team contacts the lead to qualify the business and understand their needs.
3. **Discovery Call (if needed)** — If qualified, a sales rep conducts a discovery call. A demo is offered based on complexity or request.
4. **Ongoing Trial Usage** — The buyer continues using Qoyod during the free trial. Sales team remains available for questions, setup guidance, and plan recommendations.
