# Anti-Claims — What Qoyod Does NOT Do

This is the most important file in the KB for honest marketing. It catalogs claims that are **false** about Qoyod, common misconceptions, and category boundaries the product does not cross.

**Why this exists:** when AI agents (or humans) generate content, the riskiest mistake is inventing a feature that sounds plausible. This file is the explicit "no" list — every entry here means "do not write that Qoyod does this."

Last verified: 2026-05-04.

---

## Categories Qoyod is NOT

| Claim | Reality |
|---|---|
| ❌ "Qoyod is a CRM" | Qoyod is **accounting + POS**, not a CRM. It has customer records but no pipeline, deal stages, or marketing automation. Refer customers to HubSpot or Salesforce for CRM. |
| ❌ "Qoyod is an ERP" | Qoyod overlaps with ERP in accounting/inventory/HR-adjacent features, but it is **not** a full ERP (no manufacturing BOM, no advanced supply chain, no plant maintenance modules). Don't position it as ERP-replacement. |
| ❌ "Qoyod is project management software" | The Tasks & Projects module exists for **time and cost tracking against accounting**, not as a standalone PM tool. Don't compare it to Asana/Monday/Trello. |
| ❌ "Qoyod is a banking app" | Qoyod connects to banks for reconciliation; it does not move money, issue cards, or hold balances. |
| ❌ "Qoyod is an HR platform" | Qoyod has payroll-adjacent features but is not a full HR system. For full HR, integrate with Jisr. |

## Integrations Qoyod does NOT have

These are **explicitly excluded** from the integrations list — never claim them:

- **POS apps as integrations:** Foodics, Rewaa, Loyverse, Square, Lightspeed are **competitors**, not integrations. Qoyod has its own native POS (Qoyod POS + Q.Flavours for restaurants).
- **Ads platforms:** Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok Ads are not integrated and not relevant to accounting.
- **Generic file storage:** Google Drive, Dropbox are not Qoyod marketplace integrations.
- **Slack / Teams:** No native integration. Don't claim notifications-to-Slack.
- **QuickBooks / Xero / Zoho Books:** These are competitor accounting platforms; Qoyod does not import from them automatically. Manual data import only.

## Features Qoyod does NOT have

If a customer asks for these, the answer is "not yet" or "not on roadmap":

- Cryptocurrency accounting / wallet integration
- Manufacturing BOM (Bill of Materials) with multi-level assemblies
- Advanced demand forecasting / MRP
- Subscription billing engine (Stripe Billing-style recurring billing automation beyond simple recurring invoices)
- Time-tracking with screenshots/keystroke logging (no employee-monitoring features)
- Multi-language UI beyond Arabic and English (no French, Urdu, Hindi, etc.)
- White-label / OEM resale (no rebranding the app)
- On-premise deployment (cloud only)

## Geographic / regulatory scope

- ✅ **Saudi Arabia (KSA):** primary market, ZATCA Phase 2 compliant.
- ✅ **GCC general:** UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar — operates but compliance level varies; verify specific tax regime support before claiming.
- ❌ **EU VAT / OSS:** No native support for EU VAT regimes.
- ❌ **US sales tax:** No US sales tax engine. Don't position for US market.
- ❌ **Egypt e-invoicing:** Not certified for Egypt's e-invoicing regime.

## Pricing claims to avoid

- ❌ Specific SAR or USD prices on public pages — pricing is shown post-signup, and stale numbers go viral.
- ❌ "Free forever" — Qoyod has a 14-day trial, then paid plans only. There is no free tier.
- ❌ "Cheaper than [competitor]" — never claim price superiority without a current side-by-side; competitor pricing changes constantly.

## Compliance claims to avoid without verification

- ❌ "ISO 27001 certified" — verify with security team before claiming.
- ❌ "SOC 2 compliant" — verify with security team before claiming.
- ❌ "GDPR compliant" — Qoyod is not EU-focused; don't claim GDPR posture.
- ❌ "HIPAA compliant" — out of scope; not a healthcare product.

## How to add an anti-claim

When you spot a wrong claim on the website, in a deck, or in AI output:

1. Add a row in the appropriate section above.
2. Phrase it as "❌ [false claim]" with a brief reason.
3. Open a PR — every entry should be reviewed.
4. If the claim already exists on a live page, file a ticket to fix that page.

This file is **append-only by intent**: removing an anti-claim should require strong evidence the underlying limitation has been resolved.
