### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by channel

**CAC = paid spend ÷ paid-attributed won deals.** Different from CPL — CPL is per
*lead*, CAC is per *paying customer*. Better measure of true marketing efficiency.

**Industry rule of thumb:** CAC should be ≤ 1/3 of customer lifetime value (LTV).
For Qoyod's annual SaaS: if avg LTV ≈ SAR 12K (3-year contract), healthy CAC ≤ SAR 1,000 (~$267 USD).

**Reading the chart:**
- 🟢 Channels with CAC < $300 → efficient, scale up
- 🟡 Channels $300–$1,000 → review for optimization
- 🔴 Channels > $1,000 → likely burning, audit creatives + audience

The change column shows whether CAC is rising or falling vs the prior period.
Rising CAC + rising spend = scaling out the efficient frontier (warning).
